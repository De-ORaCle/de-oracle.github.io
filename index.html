About Me.

Bukola Olapade is a passionate product designer with a flair for blending creativity with functionality. With a background in Mathematics from the University of Ibadan and years of hands-on experience, Bukola has been instrumental in building intuitive and user-centered designs for a wide range of projects. From founding Electpoll, a digital voting solution that has transformed over 20 elections, to creating sleek, user-friendly interfaces at work, Bukola's design journey is driven by a deep understanding of user needs and market dynamics. His approach to design is not just about making things look good, but making them work seamlessly for real people in real scenarios.

When Bukola isn't busy designing, he's mentoring the next generation of creatives, having led numerous workshops and events as part of the Google Developer Student Club. His toolbox includes everything from Figma to HTML/CSS/JS, which he uses to turn ideas into functional, delightful experiences. Whether it's leading a product design session or prototyping an MVP, Bukola knows how to bring a project to life with a perfect balance of fun, precision, and user-focused design thinking.
